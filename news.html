New models now out

Two hanging-type large models, whose release in Japan had long been awaited, are now out. The shade of MINOMUSHI sospensione, the largest light in the series, is 1,950 mm high, and that of the TATSUNO-OTOSHIGO light measures 1,503 mm, allowing the creation of unique IN-EI ISSEY MIYAKE light scenes in spaces with a high ceiling. These large pieces can be enjoyed in combination with the smaller models already being sold. Please stay tuned for further developments in the growing world of IN-EI ISSEY MIYAKE.
